Company

Integrix Drafting Services provides engineering and drafting support for product development, fabrication, and documentation. We work with individuals and organizations to convert concepts into manufacturable designs and clear technical deliverables.

Typical deliverables include 2D drawings, 3D models, assemblies, design reviews, and production-ready documentation.

  • Responsive communication and structured project workflows
  • Clear revision control and organized deliverable packages
  • Design intent documented for fabrication and procurement
